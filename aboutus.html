Goals of website

The goal of a book website can vary depending on its specific focus and audience, but some common goals might include:
Promoting Literature: Encouraging reading and appreciation of literature by providing recommendations, reviews, and discussions about books across various genres and topics.
Connecting Readers and Authors: Creating a platform where readers can discover new authors and books, while also providing a space for authors to promote their work and engage with their audience.
Providing Resources: Offering resources such as book summaries, reading guides, author interviews, and literary analysis to enrich the reading experience and deepen understanding of books.
Building Community: Fostering a community of book lovers who can share their passion for reading, discuss their favorite books, and connect with like-minded individuals.
